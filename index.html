Goal-Setting Tips

Make Goals SMART:

  • • Specific - Clear and detailed
  • • Measurable - Can track progress
  • • Achievable - Realistic for you
  • • Relevant - Important to your business
  • • Time-bound - Has a deadline

Examples of Good Goals:

  • • "Get 10 paying customers by month 2"
  • • "Earn $2,000 revenue in first month"
  • • "Complete business registration by week 3"
  • • "Build email list of 100 prospects"

Pre-Launch Checklist

Legal & Admin:

  • ☐ Choose business structure (LLC, etc.)
  • ☐ Register business name
  • ☐ Get required licenses/permits
  • ☐ Open business bank account
  • ☐ Get business insurance
  • ☐ Set up basic bookkeeping

Marketing & Operations:

  • ☐ Create simple website/online presence
  • ☐ Design basic logo/branding
  • ☐ Set up social media accounts
  • ☐ Create pricing structure
  • ☐ Develop service/product delivery process
  • ☐ Plan customer communication system
